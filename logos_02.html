Logos

Page 1 - 2 - 3

This page may take a little bit longer to download (sorry!), but that’s because it hosts a selection of the Allegro 1999 logo competition and since most of the images are pretty small, there’s no need for thumbnails. This competition, organized by Toni Ylisirniö, attracted a few artists. The competition had some rules, and they were for example to draw the logo in 256 colors, or in a small area. Note that all the graphics here are public domain, so you can use them as you wish in your programs.
